MACHU PICCHU

Visit the Lost City

Machu Picchu, otherwise known as the "Lost City" of the Incas, is consistently ranked one of the top travel destinations in the world. Touring this mysterious historical site will bring you face to face with unique architecture, various wildlife, and the imposing mountains of Peru.

READ MORE

Tour the Historical Ruins

The Incan citadel of Machu Picchu was ranked as the #1 Tourist Landmark in 2016 on TripAdvisor. When visiting the ruins, make note of the unique architectural characteristics of the buildings. Since Machu Picchu was never mentioned by the Incans, historians guess on the purpose of Machu Picchu.

READ MORE

See the Wildlife (Ilamas!)

During your tour of Machu Picchu, you will come into contact with many types of wildlife, including Ilamas! Other than grazing around the citadel, they also wander down the numerous Incan roads that make up the Incan Trail. Hikers can expect to see Ilamas during their trek!

READ MORE

Hike the Inca Trail

The Machu Picchu hike is very well known amongst hiking enthusiasts, in particular the Inca Trail. The trek up Macchu Picchu mountain (Montana) is also popular with those looking for a little more challenge!
